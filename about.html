Where we are

A hillside spot, with a city view


We're tucked on the edge of Cave Hill Country Park in Newtownabbey, on the north side of Belfast — close enough to the city to be an easy visit, far enough up the hill that the skyline opens out beneath you.

The site is built around four things: a hot sauna, an ice bath / cold plunge pool, a hot tub, and an outdoor wild shower — all set up so you can move easily between them.

Who's on the hill

A small, hands-on team


Hilltop Sauna is run by Jo and Rachael, a small family team who will be there to welcome you on arrival, show you around and make sure you have everything you need before leaving you to enjoy your experience at your own pace.


Built by our family from the ground up, Hilltop Sauna is a project that has been shaped by love, passion and plenty of hard work. From construction to the finishing touches, every part of the business has been built and created by family, making it a place we're incredibly proud to share with you.


If you have any questions before, during or after your visit, get in touch and one of us will be happy to help.

Before you arrive

What to bring

Everything else is taken care of — just pack these four things and you're good to go.

Swimwear

You'll be moving between the sauna, ice bath and hot tub, so something you can swim in is essential.

Two towels

One to sit on in the sauna and one to dry off with at the end of your session.

Flip flops or sliders

The ground between facilities can be wet — footwear you can slip on and off easily makes the whole experience smoother.

Something warm

A dry robe, coat or hoodie to keep you warm after your session.

Come and see for yourself

The best way to understand what makes the Hilltop special is to experience it for yourself. Whether you're visiting for relaxation with friends, solo recovery or simply some time to switch off, we'd love to welcome you.


If you have any questions before booking, just get in touch and we'd be happy to help!
