The ritual

Four steps. One view.

However you like to take it, every visit moves between heat and cold — with the hot tub and outdoor shower rounding things off. Repeat the loop as many times as you like.

Sauna · ~15 min

Heat

Settle into the sauna, let the heat get into your bones, and watch Belfast spread out below through the steam.

Ice bath · 30–60 sec

Plunge

Step into the ice bath. Short, sharp, and exactly long enough to clear your head and wake everything up.

Hot tub

Soak

Ease into the hot tub, let your muscles loosen at a slower pace, and take in the view from the warm water.

Open air

Reset

Rinse off under the outdoor wild shower before you go again. Most people do the full loop two or three times.
