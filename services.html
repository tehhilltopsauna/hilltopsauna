What's included

Sauna, ice bath, hot tub & outdoor shower

Every session — shared or private — includes the full run of facilities, all set on the edge of Cavehill with landscape views over Belfast.

The sauna

A proper hot sauna

Settle in and let the heat take over. The sauna is the heart of the ritual — the place to slow your breathing, switch off, and warm up properly before the cold hits.

Whether you're booked in with friends or on your own, it's the same routine: get hot, get cold, and repeat for as long as you like.

Inside the sauna
The ice bath

Cold plunge, properly cold

This is the part everyone talks about. Step down into the ice bath straight from the sauna — it's brief, it's bracing, and the contrast is exactly the point.

Most first-timers manage a short dip. Regulars stay in longer, but nobody needs to prove anything — even a few seconds does the job.

The ice bath
Hot tub & outdoor shower

Warm up, rinse off, repeat

The hot tub is where the ritual slows down — a long, warm soak with the Belfast skyline laid out in front of you. When you're ready to go again, the outdoor wild shower is right there to rinse off under the open sky.

  • Large hot tub with landscape views
  • Outdoor wild shower, open air
  • Included with every session
